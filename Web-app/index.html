If I can write a Poem...

A sketch hand made just for me,
A surprise that made my heart sing,
He took his time and care to see,
The moment of joy that he did bring.

With gentle strokes and raise highlight,
He captured all that made me unique,
And gifted me with this delight,
A masterpiece I'll forever keep.

I felt so honored and so blessed,
To have such a thoughtful friend,
Who celebrated me with such finesse,
And brought my happiness to no end.

For every stroke and every delicacy,
Is like a little piece of heart,
A way to say, "you are extraordinary",
And a way to make a brand new start.
